SHOWCASING TALENT: RECAP OF RTB’S TALENTS LEAGUE 2022

Written by John Doe
09 April 2024

In a spirited celebration of skill and camaraderie, Rumah Talenta BCA (RTB) recently concluded its highly anticipated event, the "Talents League 2022."


From October to November, the dormitory transformed into a bustling arena where residents showcased their talents and competed in a variety of extracurricular activities, including chess, badminton, basketball, and ping pong.


The "Talents League 2022" was a testament to the diverse talents and passions of RTB residents. Each competition brought together participants from different backgrounds, united by their shared love for sports and games. Whether they were seasoned athletes or casual enthusiasts, everyone had the opportunity to participate and contribute to the vibrant atmosphere of the event.

Throughout the "Talents League 2022," residents not only competed fiercely but also supported and cheered for their fellow participants. The event fostered a sense of unity and camaraderie within the RTB community, reminding everyone that they were part of a larger family bound together by shared interests and passions.


As the final matches concluded and champions were crowned, the "Talents League 2022" left a lasting impression on all who participated. It was more than just a series of competitions; it was a celebration of talent, sportsmanship, and community spirit. As RTB looks forward to future events, the legacy of the "Talents League 2022" will continue to inspire residents to pursue their passions and showcase their talents, knowing that they have the support and camaraderie of their fellow RTB members.
