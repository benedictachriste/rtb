RTB GOLD FESTIVAL: CELEBRATING FOR GOOD, KINGDOM TO BEHOLD!

Written by John Doe
18 February 2024

OAmidst the vibrant tapestry of cultures and traditions, Rumah Talenta BCA (RTB) illuminated with joy and excitement as it celebrated the RTB Gold Festival on February 18th, 2024.


This auspicious occasion marked not only the joyous festivities of Chinese New Year but also the commemoration of RTB's birthday, creating a jubilant atmosphere filled with camaraderie and merriment.


TThe RTB Gold Festival kicked off with a delightful array of activities, each designed to celebrate the spirit of togetherness and unity within the RTB community. From the tantalizing delights of inter-class snacks to the ceremonial cutting of the golden tumpeng, residents indulged in a culinary journey that symbolized abundance and prosperity.

Throughout the festivities, the air was filled with the melodious sounds of class performances, showcasing the talents and creativity of RTB's residents. From traditional dances to musical performances, each act illuminated the stage with energy and enthusiasm, captivating the audience and fostering a sense of pride in RTB's rich cultural heritage.


AAs the day unfolded, residents also participated in engaging ice-breaking activities, fostering new friendships and strengthening bonds within the RTB community. Laughter and joy filled the air as residents came together to celebrate their shared experiences and achievements. As the RTB Gold Festival drew to a close, residents bid farewell to yet another memorable celebration, filled with gratitude for the bonds of friendship and camaraderie that had been strengthened throughout the day.
