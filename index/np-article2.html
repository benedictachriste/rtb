Indonesia's Independence Day at RTB: A Festive Unity

Written by John Doe
13 April 2024

On the vibrant morning of August 17th, 2023, the halls of Rumah Talenta BCA (RTB) echoed with the spirited chants of "Merdeka!" as residents gathered to commemorate Indonesia's Independence Day.


The festivities commenced with a flag-raising ceremony, symbolizing the pride and unity of the Indonesian nation. RTB residents stood in solemn reverence as the national anthem echoed through the corridors, evoking a profound sense of patriotism and solidarity.


Following the ceremonial proceedings, the atmosphere buzzed with excitement as residents engaged in a series of spirited games and competitions. From traditional games like Balap Karung (sack race) and Lomba Makan Kerupuk (eating crackers competition) to modern twists on classics like Tug of War, residents of all ages enthusiastically participated, showcasing their competitive spirit and camaraderie.

As the day progressed, residents also had the opportunity to enjoy cultural performances and indulge in a sumptuous feast of traditional Indonesian delicacies. The communal dining experience fostered a sense of togetherness and camaraderie, as residents bonded over shared meals and lively conversations.


As the sun began to set, residents gathered once again for a closing ceremony, reflecting on the significance of Indonesia's Independence Day and the importance of unity in diversity. With hearts filled with pride and gratitude, they bid farewell to yet another memorable celebration, knowing that the spirit of patriotism and camaraderie would continue to thrive within the walls of RTB.
